Programme Benefits

Learn with others

You get to learn at your convenience with other students in your cohorts, lets say you missed a class; you will get the pre-recorded video of the class you missed as meeting occur via a screen sharing platform like zoom and communication is done via an online messaging platform like whatsapp. You are also attached with accountablity partners in your cohort to ensure you reach your individual and group goals.

24/7 Mentorship Support

Our mentors are always available to ensure you attain mastery of your niche. Joint platforms are created with other members of the cohort where you can ask questions and get replies on any issue you are facing while learning.

Course Certification

After the course of the program, depending on the duration it takes to complete your course; a certificate will be given to you to vet and show that you have completed your course and you are fit to take jobs and projects related to that course.

Jobs/Projects Opportunities

Our certified mentees are given projects and jobs opportunities as part of compensation for investing in themselves via our platform. These projects are from part of our in-house projects and the jobs are from legit sure listing platforms that we are connected to.
